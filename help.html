unfollowers

see who unfollowed you

To utilize unfollowers, follow these steps

Download your data
  1. access your instagram account page
  2. request a download
  3. choose your instagram account
  4. click "select types of information"
  5. scroll down, select "followers and following"
  6. set format as JSON and date range as "all time"
  7. submit request
Once you have received your data
  1. unzip the file sent to your email
  2. find your "followers_and_following" folder
  3. upload "following.json" and "followers_1.json", respectively
  4. get unfollowers
